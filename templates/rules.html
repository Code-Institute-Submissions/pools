Game Rules

The rules are simple, just enter 1 for a home win, 2 for a draw or 3 for an away win. If your answer is wrong you will have one more attempt.

Points system: If answer is correct you will receive the same number of points as the answer. 1 point is scored for a correct 2nd attempt and two incorrect guess's receives zero points.

The top ten scores in 1 player mode will be displayed on the leaderboard.
